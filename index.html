About Me

I'm a first-year Computer Engineering student at Georgia Tech, diving deep into everything from distributed systems to hardware design. I’ve always been curious about how complex systems work—what makes them tick, and how software and hardware come together to create something meaningful. Lately, I’ve been really drawn to the idea of sharing what I build, whether it's a cool project, a learning moment, or something I wish someone had explained to me earlier. My goal is to create content that not only shows my engineering journey but also helps others learn and get inspired along the way.
